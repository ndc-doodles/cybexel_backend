N-ME is an innovative app that helps you quickly find and connect with trusted local businesses near you. Whether it’s dining, home services, healthcare or shopping, everything is right at your fingertips. Launched in 2022 by Cybexel, our mission is to grow local businesses and make daily life simpler and more convenient. With real-time updates, easy booking and instant access to thousands of services, N-ME builds stronger, more connected communities.
